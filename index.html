About Our Company

Shri Narayana Electricals & Control Panel Boards is one of Bangalore's trusted manufacturers of electrical control panels. Established in 1999, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality Electrical Meter Panels, PCC Panels, MCC Panels, APFC Panels, LT Panels and customized electrical control panels for industrial, commercial and residential projects.

Our focus is on quality workmanship, reliable products, timely delivery and excellent customer service.
