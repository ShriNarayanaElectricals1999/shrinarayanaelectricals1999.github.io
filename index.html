About Our Company

Shri Narayana Electricals and Control Panel Boards is a highly respected, Class-1 state-approved electrical contractor and control panel manufacturing pioneer based in Bangalore. Established in 1999, we have spent over two and a half decades designing, engineering, and commissioning robust electrical panel boards for prominent industrial complexes, high-rise residential projects, and commercial hubs.

We take immense pride in our engineering precision, ensuring that every panel leaving our workshop meets strict national safety standards. By combining unmatched craftsmanship with high-quality raw materials, we provide customized, durable, and highly efficient power distribution structures engineered to withstand heavy, continuous industrial demands.

Heavy Duty Sheet Specifications

To ensure maximum structural integrity, durability, and robust environmental shielding, all of our panels are built using heavy-gauge metal panels. We manufacture our enclosures strictly in:

14 Gauge (Heavy Duty) 16 Gauge (Standard Heavy)
